12 Years A Slave

This is a historical drama film and a modification of 1853 life history of slaves. This film is about a New York State African American born free man. Northup was kidnapped in 1841 by two conmen, who forced and sold him into slavery under the name ‘Platt’ for 12 years. Northup was put to work on plantations in the state of Louisiana for 12 years before he was released. Northup faces the hardship of being a slave by serving under the hands of a few different slave owners. With determination, faith, will power, and courage Northup must survive and be able to endure the 12years as a slave.
